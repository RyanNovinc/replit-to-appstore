‚ö†Ô∏è Common Issues

Error: "Build failed"

Solution: Check the build logs in the Expo dashboard. Common causes: missing dependencies in package.json, incorrect bundle identifier in app.json, or invalid iOS configuration.

Error: "Invalid Bundle ID"

Solution: Make sure the Bundle ID in app.json exactly matches the one you created in App Store Connect. They must be identical.

App Rejected by Apple

Solution: Apple will email you the specific reason. Common reasons: missing privacy policy, incomplete app description, or app crashes during review. Fix the issue and resubmit.

üéâ What's Next?

After your app is approved, you can update it anytime by incrementing the version number in app.json, running a new build, and submitting again. The update process is the same as the initial submission.
